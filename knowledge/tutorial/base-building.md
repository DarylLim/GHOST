# Base Building in EVE Frontier

Smart Assemblies are player-deployed on-chain structures that form the backbone of EVE Frontier's player-driven world. Building your first base is a critical progression milestone.

## Smart Assembly Types

- **Smart Storage Unit (SSU)**: Stores items. Can have custom extensions and dApps. The most common structure
- **Smart Turret**: Automated defense. Targets hostiles in range based on owner-defined rules
- **Smart Gate**: Player-built jump gates connecting systems. Critical infrastructure for travel networks
- **Refinery**: Processes raw ore into refined materials. Essential for advanced crafting

## Building Your First SSU

1. Gather required materials (hull plates, power cells, circuit boards)
2. Open the construction menu
3. Select Smart Storage Unit
4. Choose a deployment location in space
5. Confirm the on-chain transaction (requires SUI gas)
6. The SSU is now live and visible to all players in the system

## SSU Extensions

SSU owners can deploy custom Sui Move extensions that add programmable logic — custom access control, automated trading, event logging, and more. Each SSU can also have a custom dApp URL that opens in the game's built-in browser when players press F on it.

## Strategic Considerations

- Deploy in systems with traffic for visibility
- Consider defense: place turrets near valuable assemblies
- SSUs are persistent — they exist on-chain even when you're offline
- Other players can interact with your assemblies (by design)
