# First Combat in EVE Frontier

Combat in EVE Frontier is dangerous and consequential. Your Shell can be destroyed, and death means losing everything you're carrying. Understand combat before you engage.

## Combat Basics

- **Targeting**: Select a hostile entity to target it. Your weapons need a lock before firing
- **Range**: Different weapons have different effective ranges. Stay in your optimal
- **Damage types**: Weapons deal damage to shields first, then armor, then hull. When hull reaches zero, your Shell is destroyed
- **Escape**: Warp out if you're losing. The warp alignment takes a few seconds — don't wait until it's too late

## Feral AI

Feral AI are NPC hostiles that spawn in various systems. They come in different classes:
- **Shuttles**: Weakest. Good practice targets for newcomers
- **Frigates**: Moderate threat. Can be dangerous in groups
- **Cruisers**: Serious threat. Engage only if well-equipped
- **Battleships**: Extreme threat. Avoid unless in a group

Feral AI aggression varies. Some patrol passively until provoked. Others (aggressors) will attack on sight.

## Survival Rules

1. Never fight with Crowns in your hold
2. Never fight on low fuel — you need escape options
3. Know your Shell's limits — don't engage above your class
4. Watch for additional hostiles warping in during a fight
5. If shields drop below 30%, consider warping out
6. Dock and repair after every engagement
