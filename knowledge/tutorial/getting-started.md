# Getting Started in EVE Frontier

EVE Frontier is a blockchain-based space survival game running on the Sui network. You play as an immortal consciousness uploaded into biomechanical ships called Shells. The game takes place in Stillness, the current production server.

## First Steps

When you first enter EVE Frontier, you spawn in a starter system with a basic Shell. Your immediate priorities are:

1. **Familiarize with controls**: WASD for movement, mouse for camera, F to interact with Smart Assemblies, Tab for system map
2. **Check your fuel**: Your Shell runs on fuel. Without it, you cannot warp between systems. The fuel gauge is in the top-right of your HUD
3. **Find a storage unit**: Smart Storage Units (SSUs) are player-deployed structures that act as inventory storage and service points. Fly to one and press F to interact
4. **Mine your first resources**: Target an asteroid and activate your mining laser. Ore is the foundation of EVE Frontier's economy

## Your Shell

Your Shell is your ship and your life. If your Shell is destroyed, you lose everything inside it — inventory, Crowns, and cargo. You respawn in a new starter Shell at your home point. This is permanent loss. There is no insurance.

## Key Concepts

- **Smart Assemblies**: Player-built structures deployed on-chain. Storage Units, Gates, Turrets, and more
- **Crowns**: Premium in-game currency. Valuable and dangerous to carry
- **Fuel Cells**: Required for warping between systems. Always monitor your fuel
- **Feral AI**: Hostile NPC entities that roam systems. They will attack you
