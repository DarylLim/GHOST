# Building Your Reflex Ship

The Reflex is the first advanced Shell available to players. Upgrading from your starter Shell to a Reflex is a major milestone that significantly improves your combat and exploration capabilities.

## Why the Reflex

- Higher base HP, shield, and armor values
- More cargo capacity for longer mining runs
- Better module slots for weapons and utilities
- Faster warp alignment for quicker escapes

## Requirements

To build a Reflex, you need:
1. **Refined Materials**: Hull alloy, shield emitters, drive components. All refined from mined ores
2. **A Refinery**: Either your own or access to another player's
3. **Construction Blueprint**: Available from certain in-game sources
4. **SUI Gas**: The construction is an on-chain transaction

## Build Process

1. Gather all raw ores
2. Refine ores into components at a Refinery
3. Ensure all materials are in the same Smart Storage Unit
4. Open construction menu and select Reflex Shell
5. Confirm construction transaction
6. Board your new Shell

## Tips

- Don't rush it. Losing a Reflex to carelessness is devastating
- Keep your starter Shell as a backup — you can switch
- Practice combat in your starter Shell first
- The Reflex is not invincible. It just gives you more margin for error
