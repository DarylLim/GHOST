# Leaving the Starter System

The starter system is relatively safe but resource-poor. To progress, you'll eventually need to venture into the wider universe. This is dangerous but necessary.

## When to Leave

You should be ready to leave when:
- You have a Reflex Shell (or at minimum, fully repaired starter Shell)
- You have at least 500+ fuel (enough for multiple warps and a return trip)
- You've completed your first combat encounters
- You've stored valuable items in an SSU (don't carry everything)
- You understand the warp and gate mechanics

## How to Travel

- **Warp**: Direct travel within a system. Costs fuel based on distance
- **Gates**: Player-built Smart Gates connect systems. Using a gate costs fuel. Not all systems have gates — some require discovering new routes
- **System Map**: Press Tab to see connected systems and their threat levels

## Outer System Dangers

Systems further from the starter zone have:
- More and stronger Feral AI spawns
- Rarer and more valuable resources
- Other players who may be hostile
- Fewer player-built structures (less infrastructure)

## Survival Checklist Before Warping

1. Fuel above 50%
2. No Crowns in cargo (store them first)
3. Hull fully repaired
4. Know your destination and have a return route
5. Check destination threat level on system map
