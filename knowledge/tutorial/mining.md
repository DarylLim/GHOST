# Mining in EVE Frontier

Mining is the primary resource gathering activity and the foundation of the economy. Asteroids contain various ores that can be refined into materials for construction and crafting.

## How to Mine

1. Navigate to an asteroid field (visible on your system scanner)
2. Approach an asteroid within mining range
3. Target the asteroid and activate your mining module
4. Ore is deposited directly into your cargo hold
5. When your hold is full, dock at a Smart Storage Unit to offload

## Ore Types

Different asteroids yield different ores. Richer ores are found in more dangerous systems further from the starter zones. Common ores include base metals for hull construction, while rarer ores are needed for advanced components.

## Mining Safety

Mining makes you vulnerable. Your ship is stationary and your attention is on the rocks, not the scanner. Best practices:

- **Always watch the scanner**: Hostiles can warp in at any time
- **Mine in groups if possible**: Safety in numbers
- **Keep fuel reserve**: Never mine until your fuel is empty. Keep enough for at least one emergency warp
- **Don't carry Crowns while mining**: If you die, you lose them
- **Dock frequently**: Don't accumulate too much cargo in a single run. The more you carry, the more you lose

## Refining

Raw ore must be refined into usable materials. This requires a Refinery — either your own Smart Assembly or access to another player's. Building your own Refinery is a major progression milestone.
