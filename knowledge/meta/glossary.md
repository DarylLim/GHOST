# EVE Frontier Glossary

Essential terminology for EVE Frontier players.

- **Shell**: Your ship/body. A biomechanical vessel containing your consciousness. Loss is permanent
- **Crowns**: Premium in-game currency. Valuable and lost on death
- **Smart Assembly**: Player-built on-chain structure (SSU, Turret, Gate, Refinery)
- **SSU (Smart Storage Unit)**: Storage structure. Can have custom extensions and dApp URLs
- **Smart Turret**: Automated defense platform with programmable targeting
- **Smart Gate**: Player-built jump gate connecting systems
- **Feral AI**: Hostile NPC entities. Rogue autonomous systems from a collapsed civilization
- **Aggressor**: A Feral AI that attacks players on sight (vs patrol types that ignore you)
- **Stillness**: The current production server/world of EVE Frontier
- **Warp**: Travel between locations within or between systems. Costs fuel
- **Fuel Cell**: Consumable item that replenishes Shell fuel
- **Refinery**: Smart Assembly that processes raw ore into refined materials
- **Orbital Zone**: Area around a celestial body where gameplay occurs
- **System Map**: Navigation interface showing connected systems and threat levels
- **Hull/Shield/Armor**: The three layers of Shell defense (shield first, then armor, then hull)
- **Sui**: The blockchain network that EVE Frontier runs on
- **EVE Vault**: The in-game wallet compatible with Sui Wallet Standard
- **dApp**: Decentralized application. In EVE Frontier, custom web UIs that load in the game browser
- **Extension**: A Sui Move package deployed as programmable logic on a Smart Assembly
- **On-chain**: Data or logic that exists on the Sui blockchain. Permanent and verifiable
