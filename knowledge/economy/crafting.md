# Crafting in EVE Frontier

Crafting transforms raw and refined materials into usable items, modules, and structures. It is essential for progression beyond the starter phase.

## Crafting Chain

1. **Mine** raw ore from asteroids
2. **Refine** ore into materials at a Refinery
3. **Craft** materials into items, modules, or construction components
4. **Build** final products (Shells, Smart Assemblies)

## Key Craftable Items

- **Fuel Cells**: Refueling consumables. Always in demand
- **Hull Plates**: Basic construction component. Used in everything
- **Power Cells**: Energy components for assemblies
- **Circuit Boards**: Logic components for Smart Assembly extensions
- **Shield Emitters**: Ship shield components
- **Drive Components**: Ship engine components
- **Weapon Modules**: Offensive ship equipment

## Crafting Tips

- Focus on Fuel Cells first — you always need them and they're great trade goods
- Building a personal Refinery should be a priority after your first SSU
- Keep a stockpile of common materials in your SSU for emergency crafting
- Advanced items require rare materials from dangerous systems
- Check community markets before crafting — sometimes buying is cheaper than gathering
