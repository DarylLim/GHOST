# Trading in EVE Frontier

EVE Frontier has a player-driven economy built on blockchain. All items, resources, and assets exist on-chain. Trading happens through direct exchanges, Smart Assembly services, and community markets.

## How Trading Works

- Players trade through Smart Storage Units configured for trading
- Some SSUs have custom extensions that implement automated market logic
- Direct trades happen through in-game transfer mechanics
- All transactions are on-chain and verifiable

## What Has Value

- **Fuel Cells**: Always in demand. The universal necessity
- **Rare Ores**: Found only in high-threat systems. Used for advanced crafting
- **Refined Materials**: Processed components needed for construction
- **Crowns**: The premium currency. High value, high risk to transport
- **Blueprints**: Rare construction recipes for advanced items

## Trading Safety

- Never carry more value than you can afford to lose
- Trade at defended SSUs (near turrets)
- Verify on-chain transaction details before confirming
- Be wary of trades that seem too good — EVE Frontier has scammers
- Use established community marketplaces when available

## Economic Opportunities

- Fuel delivery services (bring fuel to stranded players for payment)
- Mining and refining operations (sell refined materials)
- Transit services (build and charge tolls on Smart Gates)
- Defense services (deploy turrets for other players' bases)
