# Feral AI

Feral AI are rogue autonomous entities that inhabit the systems of EVE Frontier. They are the primary PvE threat and a constant danger in space.

## What Are Feral AI?

In EVE Frontier's lore, Feral AI are remnants of a collapsed civilization's autonomous systems. They've been running without oversight for millennia, becoming unpredictable and hostile. They patrol the void between stars, attacking anything that comes too close.

## Feral AI Classes

- **Shuttle-class**: Small, weak. Minimal threat individually. Often found in starter systems
- **Frigate-class**: Standard threat. Can deal meaningful damage. Travel in small groups
- **Cruiser-class**: Significant threat. Thick shields and armor. Can destroy a starter Shell quickly
- **Battleship-class**: Extreme threat. Do not engage solo. Can one-shot weak Shells

## Behavior Patterns

- **Patrol**: Moves through space on a route. Will not attack unless provoked
- **Aggressor**: Will attack any player on sight. Identified by scanner signature. Aggression multiplier: 2.5x effective threat
- **Swarm**: Multiple Feral AI coordinating. If you see 3+, assume they're a swarm

## Fighting Feral AI

- Match your ship class to the threat. Don't punch above your weight
- Focus fire on one target at a time
- Watch for reinforcements warping in mid-fight
- Feral AI drop loot on destruction — sometimes valuable components
