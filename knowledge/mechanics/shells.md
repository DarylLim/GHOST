# Shells

Shells are the biomechanical ships that players inhabit in EVE Frontier. Your consciousness is uploaded into a Shell, making it both your vehicle and your body. Losing your Shell means losing everything inside it.

## Shell Types

- **Starter Shell**: Given on first spawn. Basic stats, limited cargo. Disposable but free on respawn
- **Reflex Shell**: First craftable upgrade. Better HP, shields, armor, and cargo capacity
- **Advanced Shells**: Higher-tier Shells require rarer materials and more complex construction

## Shell Stats

- **Hull Points (HP)**: Base health. When this hits zero, the Shell is destroyed
- **Shields**: First layer of defense. Regenerates slowly over time
- **Armor**: Second layer. Does not regenerate — must be repaired
- **Cargo Capacity**: How much you can carry. Filling cargo does not slow you down but increases your loss risk
- **Module Slots**: Slots for weapons, mining lasers, scanners, and utility modules

## Death Consequences

When your Shell is destroyed:
- All cargo is lost permanently (ore, materials, items)
- All Crowns in your hold are lost permanently
- You respawn in a new starter Shell at your home point
- Smart Assemblies you own are NOT destroyed (they persist on-chain)
- Your wallet and on-chain assets outside the Shell are safe

This is the core tension of EVE Frontier: carrying more means risking more.
