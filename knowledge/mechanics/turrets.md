# Smart Turrets

Smart Turrets are automated defense structures that players deploy to protect areas of space. They are one of the most important defensive Smart Assemblies.

## How Turrets Work

Turrets are anchored in space and automatically engage targets based on owner-defined rules. The targeting logic is programmed via on-chain extensions — the turret's behavior is deterministic and verifiable.

## Turret Targeting

Owners can program turrets to:
- Attack all hostiles in range (Feral AI)
- Protect specific players or groups
- Engage anyone not on a whitelist
- Custom logic via Sui Move extensions

## Tactical Use

- **Mining defense**: Place turrets near popular mining zones to deter Feral AI
- **Base defense**: Surround your SSU or Refinery with turrets
- **Chokepoint control**: Place turrets at gate approaches
- **Territory marking**: Turrets signal that an area is claimed and defended

## Limitations

- Turrets have limited range — they won't protect an entire system
- They can be destroyed by sufficient firepower
- They consume resources (power/fuel) to operate
- Turret effectiveness depends on the quality of targeting logic
