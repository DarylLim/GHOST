# Construction

Construction in EVE Frontier covers building Smart Assemblies and crafting advanced Shells. All construction involves on-chain transactions on Sui.

## Construction Process

1. **Gather raw materials**: Mine ores from asteroid fields
2. **Refine materials**: Process ore at a Refinery into usable components
3. **Acquire blueprint**: Some items require specific blueprints
4. **Assemble**: Open construction menu, select the item, confirm on-chain transaction
5. **Deploy** (for assemblies): Choose a location in space to anchor the structure

## Required Materials

Each construction project requires specific refined materials:
- **Hull Plates**: Refined from common ore. Used in almost everything
- **Power Cells**: Refined from energy-rich ore. Powers assemblies and modules
- **Circuit Boards**: Refined from rare ore. Used in Smart Assembly logic cores
- **Shield Emitters**: Specialized component for ship shields
- **Drive Components**: Specialized component for ship engines

## Tips

- Start with a Smart Storage Unit — it's the cheapest and most useful first build
- Build a Refinery next to eliminate dependence on others for refining
- Save rare materials for ship construction rather than spending them on early assemblies
- Every construction transaction costs SUI gas — keep gas in your wallet
