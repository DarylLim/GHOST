# Ship Classes

EVE Frontier features multiple ship (Shell) classes, each with distinct roles and capabilities. Ship class determines your combat effectiveness, cargo capacity, and module options.

## Classes

### Shuttle
The smallest class. Low HP, minimal cargo, basic module slots. Starter Shells are shuttle-class. Fast and cheap but fragile.

### Frigate
Mid-tier combat and exploration vessel. Balanced stats across HP, shields, and armor. Good module flexibility. The Reflex is a frigate-class Shell.

### Cruiser
Heavy combat vessel. High HP and armor. Larger cargo hold. Slower to align for warp but can take significant punishment. Suitable for extended operations in hostile space.

### Battleship
The largest player-accessible class. Massive HP, shields, and armor. Heavy weapons. Very slow to warp align. Expensive to build and devastating to lose. Reserved for experienced players.

## Choosing Your Ship

Your ship class should match your activity:
- **Mining**: Frigate with cargo modules or Cruiser for bulk hauling
- **Combat**: Frigate for agility, Cruiser for sustained fights
- **Exploration**: Frigate for speed and fuel efficiency
- **Hauling**: Cruiser or Battleship for maximum cargo

Remember: a bigger ship means a bigger loss on death. Don't fly what you can't afford to lose.
