# Fuel System

Fuel is the most critical resource in EVE Frontier. Without fuel, you cannot warp, and being stranded in hostile space is a death sentence.

## Fuel Basics

- Every warp costs fuel based on distance
- Fuel is stored in your Shell's fuel tank (not cargo)
- Fuel tank has a maximum capacity that varies by Shell type
- Fuel does not regenerate — you must acquire more

## Getting Fuel

- **Fuel Cells**: Craftable items that can be consumed to refuel
- **Smart Storage Units**: Some SSUs operated by other players offer fuel services
- **Crafting**: Fuel Cells are crafted from specific refined materials

## Fuel Management

- Always know your fuel percentage before warping
- Calculate round-trip fuel before entering a new system
- Keep a fuel reserve for emergencies (at least 2 warps worth)
- If fuel drops below 20%, prioritize refueling above all else
- At 0% fuel with no Fuel Cells, you are stranded. Your options are: call for help, wait for rescue, or self-destruct

## Estimated Warps

The "estimated warps" number shows how many standard-distance warps you can perform with current fuel. This is an approximation — longer warps cost more fuel.

## Stranding

Being stranded (zero fuel, no Fuel Cells) is one of the worst situations in EVE Frontier. You cannot move, making you easy prey for any hostile that finds you. Prevention is always better than rescue.
