# Scanning

The scanner is your primary situational awareness tool. It shows nearby entities, including other players, Feral AI, asteroids, and Smart Assemblies.

## Using the Scanner

- The scanner updates in real-time showing entities within your detection range
- Entities are classified: neutral (white), hostile (red), friendly (green), structure (blue)
- You can identify ship classes from scanner data: Shuttle, Frigate, Cruiser, Battleship
- Aggressors (entities that will attack on sight) show a distinct signature

## Threat Assessment

Use scanner data to assess your situation:
- **0 hostiles**: Safe to mine, explore, or idle
- **1-2 hostiles**: Caution. Monitor their behavior. If they approach, prepare to warp
- **3+ hostiles**: Dangerous. Consider warping out immediately unless you're in a group
- **Battleship class**: Extreme danger regardless of count. Warp out

## Scanner Tips

- Check scanner regularly, especially while mining
- Hostiles can warp in at any time — a clear scan doesn't mean permanent safety
- Feral AI patrol patterns can be unpredictable
- Other players may or may not be hostile — their intentions aren't shown on scanner
