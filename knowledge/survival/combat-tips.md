# Combat Survival Tips

Combat in EVE Frontier is punishing. These tips maximize your chances of surviving.

## Before Combat

- Check fuel — you need warp-out options
- Store Crowns at an SSU — never fight with Crowns on board
- Know your Shell's HP, shields, and armor
- Identify the threat: ship class, hostile count, aggressor status

## During Combat

- **Focus fire**: Kill one target at a time. A damaged enemy still deals full damage
- **Monitor shields**: When shields hit 30%, seriously consider warping out
- **Watch for adds**: More hostiles can warp in mid-fight. Check scanner constantly
- **Keep moving**: Orbiting reduces incoming damage from turret-type weapons
- **Don't be greedy**: Winning a fight at 10% hull isn't winning — the next encounter will kill you

## The Warp-Out Decision

The hardest skill in EVE Frontier is knowing when to leave. The warp alignment window is your vulnerability:
- Start aligning BEFORE you're in critical danger
- If hull drops below 50% and hostiles are still alive, warp
- A Shell you warp out in is a Shell you still have tomorrow

## After Combat

- Dock immediately and repair
- Review what happened — why did you take so much damage?
- If you killed Feral AI, loot quickly and leave. Where there was one, there may be more
