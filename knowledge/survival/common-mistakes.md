# Common Mistakes in EVE Frontier

These are the most common ways new players die or lose progress. Learn from others' mistakes.

## The Top Mistakes

### 1. Flying with Crowns
The number one mistake. Crowns are premium currency and carrying them in your Shell puts them at risk. Store Crowns in an SSU immediately. The only time Crowns should be in your Shell is during a deliberate, planned transfer.

### 2. Running Out of Fuel
Players get absorbed in mining or exploring and forget to check fuel. When fuel hits zero, you're stranded. Stranded players are sitting ducks. Always maintain a fuel reserve.

### 3. Ignoring the Scanner
Not checking for hostiles before and during activities. Feral AI can warp in at any time. Players who don't watch scanner die to ambushes.

### 4. Fighting Above Their Class
Engaging Cruiser or Battleship-class Feral AI in a starter Shell. Know your limits. Retreat is always an option.

### 5. Not Having a Docked Backup
Carrying everything they own in a single Shell. When it dies, they lose everything. Keep reserves in SSUs.

### 6. Warping Into Unknown Systems
Jumping into a high-threat system without scouting, without sufficient fuel for return, or without a plan. Check the system map first.

### 7. AFK in Space
Going AFK while in open space. There is no safe log-off. If you need to step away, dock at an SSU or fly to a quiet corner.
